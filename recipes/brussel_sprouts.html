Brussel Sprouts

Description

"These are brussel sprouts. A very healthy vegetable high in fiber

Ingredients

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Wash Brussel Sprouts
  2. Cut Brussel Sprouts in half
  3. Season brussel sprouts with pepper and garlic powder
  4. Bake for 15 minutes at 370F
